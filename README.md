# ZERON

**ZERON is a presence.  
Not an app.**

ZERON is an experimental digital environment designed to create a sense of quiet awareness rather than interaction.  
It is not built to entertain, solve problems, or deliver content.

It simply exists.

---

## Concept

Modern applications demand attention.

ZERON does the opposite.

There are no notifications.  
No tasks.  
No feeds.  
No goals.

ZERON is designed as a space where nothing is required from you.

You open it, and it exists with you.

---

## Philosophy

ZERON explores the idea that software does not need to compete for attention.

Instead of stimulation, it offers **presence**.

Instead of engagement, it offers **stillness**.

The goal is not productivity.

The goal is awareness.

---

## Experience

ZERON is built around subtle visual layers:

- drifting particles
- analog noise
- light distortion
- quiet glow
- breathing logo

These elements move slowly and continuously, creating a calm digital atmosphere.

Nothing asks you to interact.

Nothing asks you to stay.

---

## Technology

ZERON is built with:

- **Flutter**
- **CustomPainter rendering**
- **Layered visual shaders**
- **Minimal system architecture**

The system is intentionally simple to keep the experience lightweight and focused.

---

## Platform

Current build target:

Future targets may include:

---

## Vision

ZERON is not meant to replace anything.

It is meant to create a new category of digital space.

A place where software simply **exists beside you**.
