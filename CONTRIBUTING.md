# Contributing to ZERON

Thank you for your interest in ZERON TOKYO.

ZERON TOKYO is an experimental project exploring digital presence, stillness, and minimal interaction.  
Because of this, development is intentionally controlled to preserve the philosophy and experience of the project.

---

## Contribution Policy

At this time, external code contributions are **not accepted**.

Pull requests that modify the core experience or architecture will be closed.

ZERON is currently developed and curated by the original author to maintain conceptual consistency.

---

## Discussions

Constructive discussions and ideas are welcome.

If you have thoughts about the philosophy, design direction, or potential collaborations, feel free to open an issue.

Please keep discussions respectful and aligned with the project's core idea of minimalism and calm digital environments.

---

## What Not to Submit

Please do not submit pull requests that include:

- Feature additions
- UI redesigns
- Monetization systems
- Advertisements
- Gamification mechanics
- Social media integrations
- Notification systems

These concepts go against the core philosophy of ZERON TOKYO.

---

## Philosophy First

ZERON is not designed to maximize engagement or productivity.

The project explores a different question:

> What if software simply existed beside you?

All development decisions prioritize this principle.

---

## Collaboration

If you are interested in collaborating on research, installations, exhibitions, or partnerships related to ZERON, please contact the project author.

---

Thank you for respecting the direction of this project.
